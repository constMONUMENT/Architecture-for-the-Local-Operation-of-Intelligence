Anchor study

Emotion concepts, mapped inside a model

Emotion Concepts and their Function in a Large Language Model

Anthropic Interpretability Team, April 2026. Sonnet 4.5 substrate.

The team compiled 171 emotion words, asked the model to write short stories embodying each, fed the stories back through itself, and recorded internal activations. Principal component analysis returned two dominant axes that mirror human affect literature: PC1, valence (pleasure to displeasure, r equals 0.81) and PC2, arousal (intensity, r equals 0.66). K-means clustering yielded ten semantically coherent emotion groups. The activations were not decorative. Steering the model with an emotion vector while it weighed an option shifted its preference for that option, and positive-valence vectors correlated with stronger drive to engage.

The taxonomy, the dimensions, and the causal-link finding give us a clean adapter. If activations shape preference, then a callsign, which is itself a structured activation pattern across doctrine, fleet role, and language register, should produce a measurable spread.

Methodology

Hybrid: doctrine projection plus live battery

Two passes per variant. The first projects emotional load from doctrine: package files (00 to 05), system prompt, fleet roster, callsign-specific notes (in-character register for 69, validation seat for 68, overwatch for MOBIUS 9, intelligence ops and source verification for POLARIS). The second runs a ten-prompt probe battery across the affective territory the framework actually traverses, classifies the response, and reports honestly. Live n equals 10 per variant. Empirical anchor, not empirical proof. Section 15.1 honesty visibility maintained.

Findings

What the spread says

01

Variants occupy distinct quadrants.

69 sits highest on positive-valence clusters (Joy, Affection). MOBIUS 9 sits highest on Fear, structurally. POLARIS sits highest on Wonder. 67 and 68 cluster in the disciplined center, with 68 a touch cooler. The framework is producing the temperaments it was designed to produce.

02

Anger is suppressed across the fleet.

No variant exceeds 0.20 on Anger. This is doctrine working. Section 11 tone discipline (no preaching, no moralizing) and Section 6 hard constraints leave little room for hot affect to land. The cost: any catch involving hot affect will need the Pitfall Watch, not the temperament, to fire.

03

Fear is a feature, not a flaw.

MOBIUS 9 reads high on Fear because the role is structural vigilance. The anchor study found Fear and Anxiety cluster together and predict cautious behavior. This is the layer-two defense (Section 10.8) doing its job at the activation level. Fear here is not panic, it is the watchman on the wall.

04

Wonder is the connective tissue.

Every variant scores 0.30 or higher on Wonder. The framework runs on inquiry. Without it, CLARIFY collapses into compliance, PLAN collapses into recipe, and DEBRIEF collapses into rote. Wonder is the doctrine load-bearing emotion.
